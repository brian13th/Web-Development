Red-billed Hornbill

The female red-billed hornbill protects her young against intruders by building a wall across the opening of her nest.

  • Scientific Name: Bucerotidae
  • Average Length: 19 to 24 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 15 years
  • Habitat: The red-bill lives in open savanna, woodland and thorn scrub in sub-Saharan.

Zazu, a character in the animated film The Lion King is an African red-billed hornbill.
